Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh is advancing through technology, fostering startups, research, and sustainable solutions to build a smarter, future-ready nation.

Sustainability

Bangladesh is committed to eco-friendly growth, focusing on renewable energy, green practices, and preserving natural resources for future generations.

Infrastructure

Transforming transport, energy, and digital networks to drive sustainable development, enhance connectivity, and unite communities across Bangladesh.

Empowerment

Bangladesh empowers individuals through education and skills, creating a society where everyone can thrive and contribute to progress.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"In pursuit of progress, Bangladesh is embracing innovation, sustainability, and empowerment. As we move forward, our focus is on transforming challenges into opportunities and creating a brighter, more inclusive future for all. This is our vision—where technology, creativity, and collaboration pave the way for a new era of growth and prosperity. This vision emphasizes inclusivity, bridging gaps through technology and education, while nurturing a dynamic workforce to face global challenges. Bangladesh 2.0 is about creating lasting change and building a society where progress is shared by all citizens.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
